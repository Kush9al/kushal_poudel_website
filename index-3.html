Search Engine Marketing

Search Engine Marketing (SEM), Pay-Per-Click (PPC), Google Ads, Ad words are denoted differently based on their operational methodologies. However, they refer to the same thing, i.e. to pay a search engine for clicks. In digital marketing, SEM paid strategy enables an advertiser to pay a fee each time a user clicks a text/creative design, which links to their website, displayed on the specific search engine results page (SERP). You can control how much you pay for each click and how much to allocate to your advertising campaigns.
